Major Characters in UHF

the first major character in UHF is George Newman played By "Weird Al" Yankovic. in the movie George is fired from his job, only to go to a meetup where his uncle harvey helps him out by giving him ownership of channel u62, a local UHF TV channel on the brink of collapse.

the second main character is Bob Steckler played by david bowie. Bob Is george newman's friend and helps him run the TV channel

the final main character is stanley spadowski played by michael richards. stanley is a local janitor that ends up being fired. after that george newman hires him to work at channel 62, being the actor for multiple of george newman original shows
